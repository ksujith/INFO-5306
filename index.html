Stakeholder Ecosystem

A successful event relies on a diverse network of partners and clients. This section outlines the key groups involved, from the primary client overseeing the project to the community members who bring it to life.

Primary Client

The City of Denton Parks and Recreation Department, who will oversee the project and measure its success against community goals.

Participants & Community

The runners who are our primary customers, and the Denton residents and businesses who provide support and create the event's atmosphere.

City Departments & EMS

Essential municipal partners including Police, Public Works, and Emergency Medical Services who ensure a safe and orderly event.

Partners & Volunteers

Local sponsors, a designated charity partner, and the many volunteers from universities and the community who make the race possible.

Project Scope & Deliverables

To ensure project success and prevent scope creep, we have clearly defined what is included and what is excluded. The key is to focus on delivering a high-quality inaugural half marathon.

In Scope

  • ✔ A certified 13.1-mile race route.
  • ✔ Full project plan (WBS, budget, schedule).
  • ✔ All necessary permits and insurance.
  • ✔ Event website with online registration.
  • ✔ Marketing, volunteer, and logistics plans.
  • ✔ Participant materials (bibs, shirts, medals).
  • ✔ A successfully executed race day.
  • ✔ A formal project closure report.

Out of Scope

  • ✖ Organization of other race distances (5k, 10k).
  • ✖ A large-scale post-race music festival.
  • ✖ Multi-year sponsorship agreements.
  • ✖ Direct management of charity fundraising.
